Bio

I was born in the City of Peterborough and spent my formative years there until I moved to Birmingham and Aston University to continue my studies.

I left the University with a degree in pharmacy and worked as a locum community pharmacist in the East of England, while based in the City of Norwich.

At the same time as I began my career in pharmacy, I bought my first PC, a thick textbook and taught myself my first computer language. Today I can code in numerous languages, both front- and back-end, and use both relational and non-relational databases. I am also conversant in many industry-standard software (eg Node.js) and frameworks (eg React).

I am currently living back in Peterborough again.
